Welcome to SenateTrades!

Your email has now been added to the mailing list. You can expect alerts a few times per month, but this is subject to change with trading activity. Make sure to check out our Website for a list of all previous trades, more data, and community resources.

In the meantime, follow us on Twitter and check out our Dashboard to see a history or how previous trades have performed. You should also visit the senator contact information page to let officials know your thoughts on their stock trading activity.

Each trade sends out its own email so it is possible you may get multiple emails at once, as occasionally senators will file all trades made recently on a single day. Most importantly, this is in no way investment advice. I do not suggest naively buying any stock which this program discovers. It is important to do your own due-diligence and research before making any decisions. The intention of this program is to help spur ideas for future investments, and to help you stay informed.
